about

why the world needs this site

Let's be honest. Finding vegan food can sometimes be a little challenging. You're walking down the street and all you see are fast food chains followed by a steakhouses - not ideal places to find great vegan food. Then there are restaurants that are not solely vegetarian or vegan, yet they offer great vegan options. We're here to help! We've scoured the streets to find sources of delicious vegan food. And they're all listed here!

what gets listed here

Defining dietary restrictions is not always an exact science. Some vegans are a little more flexible than others. The listings on this site meet the following requirement: absolutely no animal ingredients in the food (no milk, eggs, butter, fish oil, etc). The listings here do not necessarily suggest the following: the suggested dishes may be prepared on the same surfaces as other non-vegan food (eg: fried foods may be fried in the same fryer as fried chicken). Please take this into consideration when ordering this food items. Nontheless, this site should guide you in the right direction.
